What is DangerZone

The goal of the application is to ensure the citizenâ€™s safety when there is danger around their location or they are inside a dangerous location. The application analyzes news articles about dangerous events such as shootings, fires, earthquakes, and major accidents, using NLP (natural language programming) services to retrieve the location and compare it to the user's location, and then analyzes the danger threat. Depending on the level of risk, it will send a notification warning them about the threat extracted from the news, and it will scan the current user location and add the danger zone as a geo-fence location using geo-fencing technology. Why build this app ? because a lot of incidents happen on a daily basis that are hard to avoid because information does not get out fast and people do not have the time to check the news through social media because it takes time to do so. The idea of creating a repairable application that sends information about the danger quickly through notifications so people can take extra precaution and protect themselves so they can avoid it arose.
